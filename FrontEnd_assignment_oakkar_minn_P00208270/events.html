Upcomming Events & Workshops

Coffee Tasting Session

Date: June 2, 2025

Join us for an exclusive coffee tasting experience.

Event Details:

  • Objective: Introduce customers to different coffee.
  • Duration: 1 – 3 hours
  • Location: Inside the coffee shop (or an outdoor patio if available)
  • Capacity: 10–35 participants per session
  • Cost: Free ($20–$30 per person)

Agenda:

  1. Welcome & Introduction (20 minutes)
  2. Tasting Session (1 minutes)
    • Serve 4–5 different coffee samples (e.g., Ethiopian, Colombian, Guatemalan, Sumatra)
    • Guide participants through aroma, acidity, body, and flavor notes
    • Use a tasting wheel to help participants describe flavors
  3. Interactive Q&A (25 minutes)
  4. Closing & Special Offers (20 minutes)

Welcome to our coffee tasting event:

Additional Considerations:

  • Use cupping techniques for professional-style tasting.
  • Provide tasting cards so customers can take notes
  • Pair with small pastries to enhance flavors

Brewing Masterclass

Date: January 5, 2025

Learn the art of brewing the perfect cup of coffee.

Event Details:

  • Objective: Teach customers how to brew better coffee at home using different methods.
  • Duration: 1–1.5 hours
  • Location: Coffee shop counter or a dedicated workshop space
  • Capacity: 6–10 participants per session
  • Cost: Free or $15–$25 per person (includes coffee samples)

Agenda:

  1. Introduction (10 minutes)
  2. Live Demonstration (30 minutes)
    • Barista demonstrates each brewing method step by step
    • Participants smell and compare different grinds
  3. Hands-on Brewing (45 minutes)
  4. Taste Comparison & Q&A (25 minutes)
  5. Closing & Special Offers (20 minutes)

Welcome to our Brewing Masterclass event:

Additional Considerations:

  • Provide a printed brewing guide for participants
  • Offer a follow-up class for advanced techniques (latte art, espresso extraction)
  • Partner with a local roaster for a mini roasting demo
